Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How quickly can you respond to a security emergency?

Our emergency response team is available 24/7 and can typically respond within 15-30 minutes depending on your location. For life-threatening emergencies, always call 911 first.

Do you provide free security assessments?

Yes, we offer complimentary security assessments for all potential clients. Our experts will evaluate your property and provide detailed recommendations with no obligation.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire metropolitan area and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm coverage in your specific location.

Are your security personnel licensed and insured?

Absolutely. All our security personnel are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We maintain comprehensive liability insurance and worker's compensation coverage.

Can you integrate with existing security systems?

Yes, we can often integrate with existing security infrastructure. Our technicians will assess your current systems and recommend the best integration approach.
